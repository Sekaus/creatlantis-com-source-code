Privacy Policy of The CreAtlantis.com Web Platform

Last Updated: May 22, 2026

1. Data Handling and Your Privacy

1.1 General Information

The administrators of this website prioritize user data security. We will NOT disclose sensitive information—such as credit card details, addresses, emails, or passwords—to third parties without legal cause or specific user responsibility.

We do NOT use cookies for unnecessary tracking. We utilize Sessions, which are temporary data stores on both your device and our server, primarily to maintain your login status.

Note: Sessions expire after 10 minutes to 24 hours of inactivity. We strongly suggest manually logging out if you plan to be offline for extended periods.

1.2 Data Storage Services

  • Public Data: Shared content (text, images) is forwarded to Amazon S3 for public access.
  • Private Data: Sensitive account information is stored in our private data store and is NOT shared.
  • Statistics: We collect activity history (clicks, faves, comments) for statistical purposes. This does NOT include personal identifiers like gender, age, or geography.

2. Data Service Categories

2.1 Public Data

Public data includes user posts, journals, and images. These are stored on Amazon S3 and are read-only for others; only the owner or admins may edit or delete them. No sensitive personal data is stored in these public directories.

2.2 Private Data

Private data is used strictly for identifying account ownership. It is stored on a separate, high-security server accessible only by authorized admins. The admins will NEVER ask for your password. Never share your password with anyone.

3. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to change this policy at any time to better fit the site's needs. While significant changes may be announced via pop-ups or email, it is the user's responsibility to check this page regularly for updates.
